ABOUT

Sci4Teens is an organization that fosters an interest in STEM and science communication for teens globally through our three initiatives: contests, workshops, and a newsletter.

We have opened submissions for our third writing contest. Currently, Sci4Teens hosts workshops and is leading the newsletter initiative!

Workshops


We host a variety of STEM-related workshops, aimed at introducing new topics in science & science communication to our audience. Workshops can be used for inspiration on what to write for the contest!
