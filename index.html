ABOUT

Sci4Teens is an organization that fosters an interest in STEM and science communication for teens globally through our three initiatives: the writing contest,workshops, and a newsletter.

Currently, we have opened submissions for our first writing contest, are hosting about two workshops a month, and are in the process of starting up our newsletter initiative!

OUR INITIATIVES



Workshops


We host a variety of STEM-related workshops, aimed at introducing new topics in science & science communication to our audience. Workshops can also be used to brainstorm/learn about what to write for the contest!
