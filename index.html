ABOUT


Sci4Teens is an organization that fosters an interest in STEM and science communication for teens globally through our three initiatives: the writing contest,workshops, and a newsletter.

Currently, we have opened submissions for our first writing contest, are hosting about two workshops a month, and have started up our newsletter initiative!

OUR INITIATIVES



Workshops


We host a variety of STEM-related workshops, aimed at introducing new topics in science & science communication to our audience. Workshops can also be used to brainstorm/learn about what to write for the contest!

Contest


We will be accepting blog-style articles explaining a STEM-related topic! Submissions for the first contest are due on September 5, 2020 at 11:59 PM EST. Check out the contest page for more information, the rules, and the submission form link!
