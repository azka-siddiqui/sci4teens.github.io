ABOUT


Sci4Teens is an organization that fosters an interest in STEM and science communication for teens globally through our three initiatives: contests, workshops, and a newsletter.

Currently, we have opened submissions for our second writing contest, are hosting a workshop every month, and are running our newsletter initiative!

INITIATIVES



Workshops


We host a variety of STEM-related workshops, aimed at introducing new topics in science & science communication to our audience. Workshops can also be used to brainstorm/learn about what to write for the contest!
