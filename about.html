ABOUT




Mission & Vision

At Sci4Teens, we realize that many teens across the globe are passionate about STEM, but need futher guidance on how to communicate their research or their understanding of a certain topic. This is why Sci4Teens creates equal opportunities for students looking to pursue STEM and science communication through our writing contest, workshops, and newsletter. We hope that through this organization, more teens will understand the importance of communicating their work, and can apply these skills in future careers.

Through Sci4Teens, she hopes to encourage fellow teens to get involved with science communication and to inspire them to enter several other STEM-related organizations and competitions. She hopes to incorporate several STEM-related workshops and panels through the organization, and to create a tight-knit community of STEM lovers.

FAQ

You may work either individually or in teams, with the maximum team size being three people.
Your piece can be a blog-style article about any subtopic that you are passionate about/want to explore within biology, environmental science, physics, mathematics, computer science, and engineering!
We aim to host around 2 workshops per month. Keep an eye out for the RSVP forms if you'd like to attend, and fill out our workshop interest form if you're interested in hosting one!
Workshops will present STEM/Science Communication topics that are beneficial for both the contest and your future!
Feel free to contact a mentor - the list of mentors is within the mentors page on this website!
You can send an email at sci4teens@gmail.com with your question.
